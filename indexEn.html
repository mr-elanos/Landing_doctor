Benefits of working with me

Short term

One consultation is usually enough to diagnose and form a treatment plan.

Safety

I use only evidence-based therapies by using practical recommendations of the American Psychiatric Association (The American Psychiatric Association Practice Guidelines).

Impartiality

It is psychological problems, and not nationality, social status, sexual preferences and political views that matter and determine the vector of therapy.

Stages of cooperation

1

You contact me via Telegram, Viber, call or fill out the feedback form to make an appointment for an initial consultation

2

As part of the initial consultation, we diagnose your condition and determine how exactly I can help you, choose a treatment plan

3

If you agree to the proposed treatment plan, we begin cooperation

4

End therapy when your problem is solved
